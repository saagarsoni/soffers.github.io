    About Us

    We're big fans of home-sharing and dedicated to offering quality rooms at wallet-friendly prices.InduPrabha homestay is usually a room or a property that we opened to a visitor on daily rent. Ideally, we providing you a home far from your home, however at all times there is a host who is present.Located in Dhar, near Indore,Ujjain, and Meghnagar Station. Induprabha provides accommodation with free private parking, wifi, and a morning-evening breakfast facility. Our place is around 38.5 km from Mandav and 63.5 km from Indore
