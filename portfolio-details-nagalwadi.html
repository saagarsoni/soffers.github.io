Details of Nagalwadi

The temple of Baba Bhilat Dev on the holy Tapodhara, situated on the summit of the lush green hills of Satpura, famous as a historical temple across the country, is the center of faith of countless devotees. This temple is in the Nagalwadi region. The newly built grandiose monastery with huge new carvings captures itself. People claim that this temple is full of miracles and will fulfill the wishes of all the devotees. The history of Baba Bhilat Dev is about 818 years old. Baba Bhilat Dev, considered to be the Varadaputra of Lord Shiva, was born in the Gawli family of father Railan mother Medambai in Rolgaon Patan of Harda district. Baba surprised the family and the villagers with his miraculous pastimes in his childhood. Tantra education-initiation of child Bhilat Dev is considered to be done under the supervision of Lord Shiva and Parvati.
